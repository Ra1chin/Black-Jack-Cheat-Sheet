What is a Blackjack Cheat Sheet?

Blackjack Rules: A Quick Recap

Before using a cheat sheet, it's essential to understand the basic rules of Blackjack:


  • The Goal: Get as close to 21 as possible without going over (busting).

  • Card Values: Number cards (2-10) are worth their face value, face cards (King, Queen, Jack) are worth 10, and Aces can be worth 1 or 11.

  • Game Flow: Each player receives two cards, the dealer gets one face-up and one face-down card. Players can hit, stand, double down, split, or surrender based on their hand.
